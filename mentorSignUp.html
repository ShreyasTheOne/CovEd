Mentor Registration


This time, CovEd India is partnering with

schoolhouse.world

 towards our shared goal of increasing educational access across the nation.

As soon as COVID-19 hit, schools transitioned to online learning and students around the world craved more social interaction. Sal Khan, founder of Khan Academy, saw that access to personalized and personal help was more inequitable than ever. This urgency, along with his vision of a peer coaching platform originating from his 2012 book, The One World Schoolhouse, prompted him to start schoolhouse.world. Although set up as a separate non-profit from Khan Academy, the two platforms are complementary.

Coved India mentors who join the schoolhouse.world platform will (1) be invited to a community event with Sal Khan, Founder of Khan Academy, on March 24th, (2) receive special recruiting opportunities from private employers across India, and (3) have an opportunity to help to further build and expand the schoolhouse.world platform for India.
