Who We Are
We are a growing community of over 100 students from IIT's, IISc, AIIMS and other prestigious intitutes across the nation. Many of us have achieved top ranks in the previous years' examination and are eager to share our experience with you. Our sole target to is to help as many students as possible and provide them with a resource they can use to keep their education uninterrupted.
What We Want
The sudden closure of schools due to the COVID-19 pandemic has left thousands of kids with no reliable resource to continue their education. Many of them relied on extra support such as in person doubt solving sessions to perform well in school, but donâ€™t have access to that anymore.

To provide academic as well as moral support to student, we have started an initiative called CovEd. We basically connect these school students to college students who will act as virtual mentor for them to facilitate their learning process in these tough times.
APPLYING AS A STUDENT
Fill the form
STEP 1
To register as a student, fill the form available here or on the Facebook (CovEd India) and Instagram (covedindia) pages of CovEd India. The mentors consist of students from IITs/IISc/AIIMS and various top colleges across India.
Wait for matching
STEP 2
On the basis of your stream, you will be matched with an appropriate mentor. This will take a day or so.
Contact the mentor or vice-versa
STEP 3
Your contact details will be shared with the mentor. To avoid breach of their contact details, mentors will contact mentees.
Keep in mind
STEP 4
This is a social initiative and the mentors are working for your help. Keep in mind to ask for sincere help only. This initiative is for those in need. We are in this together.
Ask for help
STEP 5
Once you are contacted by your mentor over mail, WhatsApp or facebook, ask them for your help. Don't shoot a lot of questions at once and be polite. Help is on way!
APPLYING AS A MENTOR
Fill the form
STEP 1
To register as a mentor, contact on messages over here or on the Facebook (CovEd India) and Instagram (covedindia) pages of CovEd India. We mentors consist of students from IITs/IISc/AIIMS and various top colleges across India. Join the community, help students. This is a social initiative.
Wait for matching
STEP 2
On the basis of your stream, you will be matched with an appropriate mentee. This will take a day or so.
Contact the mentee(s)
STEP 3
Your contact details will not be shared with the mentee to avoid breach of your contact details. Instead, the mentees' details will be shared with you. You need to contact them first.
Help them
STEP 4
Once you contact your mentee(s), they'll ask you for your help. Help them in the way you can and if any problems arise, share them on the main mentors group.
