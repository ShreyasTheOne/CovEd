Who We Are
We are a growing community of over 800+ students from IITs, IISc, AIIMS and other prestigious institutes across the nation. Many of us have achieved great results and ranks in previous years' examinations and are eager to share our experience with students in need. Our sole target is to help as many students as possible and provide them with a resource they can use to keep their education uninterrupted. We ran our initial initiative in the summer of 2020 as a one-to-one mentoring progra. We are now expanding so that we can provide better incentives to our mentor community. Apart from the main resource we provide, we have also compiled a list of free online educational courses under our resource tab which you can use for your benefit.
What We Want
The sudden closure of schools due to the COVID-19 pandemic has left thousands of kids with no reliable resource to continue their education. Many of them relied on extra support such as in-person doubt solving sessions to perform well in school, but donâ€™t have access to that anymore.

We aim to provide academic support and increase access to educational resources for low-income K-12 students during the COVID-19 school closures. Previously, we connected 700+ K-12 students with undergraduate mentors across India. Students who act as a virtual mentor help facilitate their learning process in these tough times.
APPLYING AS A STUDENT
Fill the form
STEP 1
To register as a student, fill the form available here.
Wait for matching
STEP 2
On the basis of your stream, you will be matched with an appropriate mentor. This will take a day or so.
Contact the mentor or vice-versa
STEP 3
Your contact details will be shared with the mentor. To avoid breach of their contact details, mentors will contact mentees.
Ask for help
STEP 4
Once you are contacted by your mentor over mail, WhatsApp or facebook, ask them for your help. Don't shoot a lot of questions at once and be polite. Help is on way!
APPLYING AS A MENTOR
Fill the form
STEP 1
To register as a mentor, you can fill out the form here, email us at covedindia@gmail.com. We mentors consist of students from IITs/IISc/AIIMS and various top colleges across India. Join the community, help students. This is a social initiative.
Wait for matching
STEP 2
On the basis of your stream, you will be matched with an appropriate mentee. This will take a day or so.
Contact the mentee(s)
STEP 3
Your contact details will not be shared with the mentee to avoid breach of your contact details. Instead, the mentees' details will be shared with you. You need to contact them first.
Help them
STEP 4
Once you contact your mentee(s), they'll ask you for your help. Help them in the way you can and if any problems arise, please email us at covedindia@gmail.com.
